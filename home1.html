It was founded by Mrs Kemi Salako in the year 1994.

It was first located at Jerico before moving to Ologuneru.

Mrs Kemi Salako founded THE CROWN COLLEGE 10 years after moving to Ologuneru.

Mrs Kemi Salako started THE CROWN COLLEGE June 20, 1994 after celebrating her 40th birthday.

The School was the first to be in Ologuneru as at that time.

Mrs Kemi Salako made sure that all her student receive addmission immediately after writing their papers.

Mrs Kemi Salako has been a very good mother to all the students.

THE CROWN COLLEGE is painted green.

While KINDERCARE NUR/PRY SCHOOL is painted yellow.
